## 摘要
基于可验证奖励的强化学习（RLVR）已成为扩展大语言模型（LLMs）推理能力的一种有前景的范式。然而，二元验证奖励的稀疏性常常导致效率低下和优化不稳定。为了稳定训练，现有方法通常相对于参考策略施加词元级别的约束。我们发现这种约束不加区分地惩罚偏差；当策略试图超越参考时，这可能会翻转验证器确定的优化方向，从而抑制收益。为解决这一问题，我们提出单向策略优化（OWPO），一种基于解耦优化方向与更新幅度原则的方法。在OWPO中，验证器决定更新方向，而参考策略仅用于调整更新幅度。具体而言，OWPO应用非对称重加权：对劣偏差（策略落后于参考）执行加速对齐，对优偏差（策略超越参考）执行收益锁定。此外，通过引入迭代参考更新，OWPO产生一种“棘轮效应”，持续巩固收益。实验结果表明，OWPO优于强基线方法，包括DAPO、OPD和MOPD，打破了固定先验的瓶颈，实现了无需依赖外部参考模型的持续自我进化。

## Abstract
Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) has become a promising paradigm for scaling reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models (LLMs). However, the sparsity of binary verifier rewards often leads to low efficiency and optimization instability. To stabilize training, existing methods typically impose token-level constraints relative to a reference policy. We identify that such constraints penalize deviations indiscriminately; this can flip verifier-determined direction when the policy attempts to outperform the reference, thereby suppressing gains. To resolve this, we propose One-Way Policy Optimization (OWPO), a method based on the principle of decoupling optimization direction from update magnitude. In OWPO, the verifier dictates the update direction, while the reference policy serves only to adjust the magnitude. Specifically, OWPO applies asymmetric reweighting: it performs Accelerated Alignment for inferior deviations (where the policy lags behind the reference) and Gain Locking for superior deviations (where the policy surpasses the reference). Furthermore, by incorporating iterative reference updates, OWPO creates a ``Ratchet Effect'' that continuously consolidates gains. Experimental results demonstrate that OWPO outperforms strong baselines, including DAPO, OPD, and MOPD, breaking the bottleneck of fixed priors to enable continuous self-evolution without reliance on external reference models.

## 2. 方法论：核心思想、关键技术细节

### 核心思想
- **原则**：将优化方向与更新幅度解耦。验证器（及其优势信号）决定更新方向，参考策略仅调节词元级的更新幅度。
- **实现方式**：通过非对称重加权（Asymmetric Reweighting）作用于标准的PPO/DAPO剪辑目标。

### 关键技术细节
1. **方向偏差（Directional Deviation）**：
   - 定义 \(\delta_t = \text{sgn}(A_t) \cdot \log\frac{\pi_\theta(y_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\text{ref}}(y_t|s_t)}\)
   - \(\delta_t > 0\) 表示“优偏差”（策略在正确方向上超越参考）；\(\delta_t < 0\) 表示“劣偏差”（策略落后于参考）。
2. **单向权重（One-Way Weight）**：
   - \(w_t = \text{sg}[\text{clip}(\exp(-\delta_t), \epsilon_{\text{low}}, \epsilon_{\text{high}})]\)
   - \(\delta_t < 0 \Rightarrow w_t > 1\)：**加速对齐（Accelerated Alignment）**，增大梯度权重，快速纠正劣偏差。
   - \(\delta_t > 0 \Rightarrow w_t < 1\)：**收益锁定（Gain Locking）**，减小梯度权重，降低方差，保护来之不易的优偏差。
3. **目标函数**：
   - 最大化 \(J_{\text{OWPO}}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}\left[ \sum_t w_t \cdot \min\left(r_t(\theta)A_t, \text{clip}(r_t(\theta), 1-\epsilon, 1+\epsilon)A_t\right) \right]\)
4. **迭代自进化（Ratchet Effect）**：
   - 每 \(K\) 步将参考策略硬替换为当前策略：\(\pi_{\text{ref}} \leftarrow \pi_\theta\)，形成棘轮效应，持续巩固提升。
5. **动态课程采样**：
   - 训练中活跃样本数从组大小 \(G\) 线性衰减到 \(1\)，逐渐从探索过渡到利用。

### 理论分析要点
- 局部等效于一个方向感知的正则化项 \(\Omega(\theta) = \mathbb{E}\left[\sum_t |A_t| \cdot \psi(\delta_t)\right]\)，其中 \(\psi'(\delta)=w(\delta)-1\)。
- 有效进展系数 \(g_{\text{OWPO}}(\delta)=w(\delta) \ge \epsilon_{\text{low}} > 0\)，保证**永不发生方向翻转**。
- 方差有界：\(\epsilon_{\text{low}}^2 \mathbb{E}[\|G_t\|^2] \le \mathbb{E}[\|w_t G_t\|^2] \le \epsilon_{\text{high}}^2 \mathbb{E}[\|G_t\|^2]\)，优偏差条件下进一步降低。
